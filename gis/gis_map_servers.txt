===== Raster Datasets =====

  * [[https://www.faa.gov/air_traffic/flight_info/aeronav/digital_products/vfr/|FAA Sectionals]]
    * GeoTIFF, GeoPDF, no processing needed
  * LANDSAT Data
    * Raw pixel data in GeoTIFF, needs processing to turn into pretty pictures
    * [[https://earthexplorer.usgs.gov/|USGS EarthExplorer]] - needs free account
    * [[https://registry.opendata.aws/usgs-landsat/|AWS S3]] - requires payment
  * USGS Topo Maps
    * [[https://prd-tnm.s3.amazonaws.com/index.html?prefix=StagedProducts/Maps/USTopo/PDF/|AWS S3]] - no payment needed
  * USFS Topo Maps
    * https://data.fs.usda.gov/geodata/rastergateway/states-regions/states.php
  * DTED/DEM Terrain Data
    * [[https://earthexplorer.usgs.gov/|USGS EarthExplorer]] 
      * DTED0, DTED1, DTED2
    * [[https://www.usgs.gov/3d-elevation-program/about-3dep-products-services|NationalMap DEMs]]
      * Up to 1m terrain resolution (chonky set)
  * [[https://visibleearth.nasa.gov/collection/1484/blue-marble|NASA Blue Marble]]
    * JPEG in equidistant rectangular


===== Vector Datasets =====
